ChromeTunes

ChromeTunes is inspired by music creation and the neurological phenomenon Chromesthesia, which occurs in a small percentage of the general population. Chromesthesia arises when stimulation of one pathway associated with a sensory experience overlaps and involuntarily triggers another sensory pathway. In this case, certain tonal sounds produce perceptions of color.

ChromeTunes generates sound programatically using JavaSript's Web Audio API. It uses different waveform frequencies to change the tone of sound, and different wave shape types to produce different sound textures.
